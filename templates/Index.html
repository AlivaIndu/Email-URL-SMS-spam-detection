Learn How To Identify Phishing Attacks

Cybercriminals use fake emails, websites, SMS messages, and social engineering tricks to steal passwords, banking details, and personal information. Understanding phishing is the first step toward staying safe online.

Fake Emails

Attackers often impersonate banks, companies, or trusted services to trick users into clicking malicious links or sharing sensitive information.

Suspicious Links

Phishing websites may look legitimate but contain fake URLs designed to steal login credentials, payment details, or personal data.

SMS Scams

Fraudulent SMS messages often create urgency, asking users to click dangerous links or verify sensitive account information immediately.

How To Stay Safe

Always verify URLs before entering passwords or personal information.

Avoid clicking suspicious links from unknown emails or SMS messages.

Check for spelling mistakes, urgent language, and fake domains.

Use phishing detection tools before trusting suspicious content.
